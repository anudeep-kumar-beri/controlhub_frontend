# Balance Sheet & Financial Reports Guide

## What is a Balance Sheet?

A **Balance Sheet** is a financial snapshot showing what you own (assets), what you owe (liabilities), and what's left over (equity) at a specific point in time.

### The Golden Equation:
```
Assets = Liabilities + Equity
```
or
```
Net Worth (Equity) = Assets - Liabilities
```

## Understanding the Reports

### When to Use Each Report:

**Balance Sheet**: 
- "How much am I worth RIGHT NOW?"
- Snapshot at a specific date

**Income Statement**: 
- "Did I make or lose money this month/year?"
- Performance over a period

**Cash Flow Statement**: 
- "Where did my money come from and go?"
- Money movement over a period
